# Account Deletion Policy

At [TenPowerfulSecret], we respect your right to privacy and allow you to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

## Steps to Request Account Deletion:

1. Email us at **vinworldproject@gmail.com** with the subject "Account Deletion Request."
2. In your email, include the following:
   - The email address associated with your account.
   - Any other information required for verification.

3. We will process your request and notify you once the account has been deleted.

## Data Deletion:

When you request to delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:
- Your personal information (name, email).
- Access to all paid content.
- Payment history, except where required by law.

## Data Retention:

Certain data, such as transaction history or legally required records, may be retained for a period of [30 days] to comply with legal obligations. After this period, this data will also be deleted.
